Step 1 - Fabrication of Parts  

21.1.1 � Fabricate the strake parts shown in figure 2 and the M-25 drawing.

21.1.2 � Layup 1 ply BID on each side of all parts. Lay flat for cure

21.1.3 � Cut and join foam to make the upper and lower skins. Suggested that you cut the skins oversize.

 

Step 2 - Jigging and Assembly of Parts

21.2.1 � Fabricate a jig table for underneath the strake (48" x 60" recommended)

21.2.2 � Level the fuselage, then position jig table underneath spar per plans. Bondo the jig table into position

21.2.3 � Bondo 55 inch tapered board to set the 17.4 inch waterline

21.2.4 � Trial fit bottom skin and baffles, using nails to hold in place

21.2.5 � Drill inch and a quarter hole shown on page 21�2, and disassemble parts

21.2.6 � Lay bottom foam on table, topside up, and glass 1 ply BID at 45� to the leading edge

21.2.7 � After cure, turn over and mark 6 lines, 1" apart, and parallel to leading edge

21.2.8 � Cut foam along these lines, making sure not to go through glass below
