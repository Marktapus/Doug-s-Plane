TIPS / WHAT I DID...

  • Opened LG holes after forward face layup (drilling from between bulkheads), then again after layup between bulkheads (drilling from front).

 

 

Step 1 - Landing Gear Bulkhead Reinforcements  

9.1.1 � Reinforcement Layup on foward face of foward bulkhead, 5 plies BID (Fig 1). (1st paragraph � Step 1) - Followed the recommendation of Bernard Siu and layed each ply individually rather than create the template/tape per plans.  My experience thus far is that the wax paper method becomes a little more difficult when you introduce complex contours.  Marked the area to be glassed, then used duct tape to protect edges.  Cut ~18" x 24" (At the furthest part of the angled cut - see picture to left.  This gave me more than enough material to work with.) BID pieces.  Epoxied surface, then applied dry cloth (with assistance from daughter).  Once all layers were done, I used electric shears to cut along the outline.  Removed duct tape, and worked edges down with brush/epoxy.  Peel ply with mix of Dacron and plastic table cover material (cheap substitute for dacron, and works great on flat surfaces!).  After both sides done, rough sanded surfaces to prep for future finish work.    Step Done  

9.1.2 � Reinforcement Layup between bulkheads, 5 plies BID (Fig 2). (2nd paragraph � Step 1) - Since this shape is less complex than step 9.1.1, I plan on using templates for the layup.  In preparation, I ensured that all surfaces were properly sanded.

9.1.3 � After cure, open holes. (1st and 2nd paragraph � Step 1) - 

9.1.4 � 2 ply BID over NACA scoop (Fig. 3). (3rd paragraph � Step 1) - Prepped area by shaping corners and microing around all corners to insure glass would conform, then glassed per plans (sandwich between wax paper and cut notches at intersection of corners). Completed during Chapter 7    Step Done  

9.1.5 � 3 ply BID over Rear LG Bulkhead, Side, and Firewall (Fig. 4). (4th paragraph � Step 1) - Per plans - Make a good template, then sandwich plies between wax paper and cut.  Place into position starting from center of side and working out towards Firewall and LG Bulkhead. Completed during Chapter 7   Step Done 

9.1.6 � 3 ply BID from LG hardpoint to Firewall hardpoint (Fig. 5). (5th paragraph � Step 1) - Per plans, similar to 9.1.5. Completed during Chapter 7   Step Done -
